About this Site

Hello and welcome to the webstie dedicated to showing off my dog Augie! This is a practical test of my newly aquired HTML & CSS skills, but it's also a great way to get to know an awesome dog.The main goal of this web page is to flex my budding skills and gain feedback on the end result.

Who is Augie?

Augie is a wonderful dog that my girlfriend and I adopted from the ASCPA in February of 2020. While we were innitially looking for a retriever, Augie's cute face and willingness to be loved drew us in. The first day we met him, we walked around Petco for 2 full hours just to spend time with him. After that walk (and a short nap on his part at our feet in the middle of the store) we knew he was the dog for us!
