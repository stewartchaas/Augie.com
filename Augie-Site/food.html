What Food Does Augie Like?

When we first adopted Augie, he was eating Whole Hearted Chicken and Rice recipe from Petco. Nowadays we have him on the Salmon and Peas recipe because Salmon is a heart-healthy way to keep his coat healthy and his joints happy. This is important to us because we've heard tell of dogs of Augie's breeds can sometimes have issues with their joints later in life.
We know this brand isn't the best posible brand though, so we're looking for alternatives!
